Full Stack Web Developer

Learn to design and develop powerful modern web applications that form the foundation for the apps, websites, and systems that people and businesses use every day.

The Full Stack Developer is a profession increasingly in demand by startups. And for good reason, this versatile profile is capable of managing a web development project from A to Z, or rather from Back End to Front End!

The job does not necessarily require you to be an expert in the Front-end and the Back-end, but rather to be able to work on both parts and therefore to fully understand the issues and objectives of each. This dual skill explains why this hybrid profession is very popular with startups today.

About

Full-stack developers can quickly adapt to any situation that may arise in a team, for example, replacing a team member, adapting to changing requirements for a product, applying extensive knowledge to develop a product, etc. They know how to deal with an unpredictable scenario during the production stage by remaining agile and adaptable.
